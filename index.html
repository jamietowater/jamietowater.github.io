About

Clear systems.
Humane design.

I’ve spent 15+ years in enterprise IT — largely in healthcare and data-heavy environments — growing from analyst and support work into database administration, senior SQL development, and modern data engineering patterns.

My foundation is practical and production-focused: SQL Server, ETL/ELT, data quality, performance tuning, and dependable delivery. I’ve built pipelines that move data from SQL to Parquet to S3, explored AWS Iceberg architectures for large-scale table migration, and optimized dbt/Snowflake workloads to reduce cost by roughly 40%.

Outside of enterprise data, I build like an inventor. VERA is my most ambitious concept so far — an accessibility-forward nail-care device built around a nail-only interface, dust-managed design, repeatable finished edges, and a deeper mission around independence, dignity, and safer self-care.

My direction now is to combine senior data engineering, modern tooling, prototyping, product invention, and immersive demo storytelling into work that stays calm under pressure — stable, correct, and genuinely useful.

VERAInclusive DesignSafety LayersSealed CartridgeFinish Polish
ConceptA handheld or guided-use system focused on safer nail reduction and edge finishing without letting soft tissue reach the working zone.

EngineeringPatent-style figures, BOM research, motion testing, removable cartridge concepts, vacuum exploration, and fast prototype iteration — all anchored in layered safety rather than cosmetic novelty.
